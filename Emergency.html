Reporting Emergencies

To report an emergency situation on the WPI Campus, immediately implement the following steps:


  • Call WPI Police immediately at 508-831-5555 or Campus phone extension 5555
  • If phones are down, find transportation or send a runner to notify WPI Police, located at Founders Hall, 26 Boynton Street (lower level).
  • Give your name, location, and emergency
  • Inform WPI Police if you need an ambulance
  • Always stay calm

Active Shooter

Evacuate if possible:

  • Have an escape route in mind
  • Leave your belongings behind
  • Keep your hands visible to the responding police officers

Hide Out:

  • Hide in an area out of the active shooter's view
  • Block entry to your hiding place and lock doors
  • Silence cell phones

Take Action:

  • Only when your life is in imminent danger
  • Attempt to incapacitate the shooter
  • Act with physical aggression. Throw items at the shooter if possible
